About Me

I am a professional freelance artist with a Bachelor's of Art Degree from Cal State Fullerton, based in Huntington Beach, CA. My current series is realistic aboriginal portraitures drawn with a ball-point pen, which are then "dressed" in acrylic paint, hand-painted in a block print pattern. My style is heavily influenced by Street Art, Japanese wood block, and Gustav Klimt. I am taking my passion and skills in Illustrative Art and my love and excitement for Technology into a new career direction in Web Pogramming and Design.


"When you take technology and mix it with art, you always come up with something innovative." -Robert Rodriguez
